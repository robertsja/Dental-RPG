Welcome to Dental RPG

This is a rock paper scissors format of game.
In this game you will be directed to the menu. You can pick to play;
Best of 5: whoever wins the most rounds out of 5 wins
Endless: Keep going until you input 0 to stop endless
High scores: Check to high score

Game modes:
Normal: Select between 3 options. 
	Tooth Brush - Beats Plaque, loses to Bad breath, draws to gum disease
	Mouth Wash - Beats Bad Breath, loses to gum disease, draws to plaque
	Dental Floss - Beats Gum disease, loses to Plaque, draws to bad breath

Complex: Select between 5 options
	Tooth Brush - Beats Plaque & Bad Breath
		      Loses to Gum Disease & Cavities
		      Draws to Yellow Teeth

	Mouth Wash - Beats Bad Breath & Gum Disease
		     Loses to Cavities and Yellow Teeth
		     Draws against Plaque
	
	Dental Floss - Beats Gum Disease & Cavities
		       Loses to Yellow Teeth & Plaque
		       Draws to Bad Breath
	
	Fillings - Beats Cavaties & Yellow Teeth
	 	   Loses to Plaque & Bad Breath
		   Draws to Gum Disease
	
	Whitener - Beats Yellow Teeth & Plaque
		   Loses to Bad Breath & Gum Disease
		   Draws to Cavities
